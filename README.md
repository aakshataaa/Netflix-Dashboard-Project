Netflix Data Analytics Dashboard

Project Overview
This project analyzes the Netflix Titles Dataset to uncover insights about content distribution, genre trends, ratings, and country-wise production using SQL and Tableau.
The objective was to transform raw data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualization and KPI dashboards.

Business Problem
. With thousands of titles available on Netflix, understanding content trends is crucial for:
. Identifying dominant genres
. Analyzing content growth over time
. Evaluating country-wise production
. Understanding audience-targeted ratings

This dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions regarding content strategy.

Tech Stack
. SQL – Data cleaning & querying
. Tableau – Interactive dashboard & visualization
. CSV Dataset – Netflix Titles dataset

 Dataset Information
. Dataset: Netflix Titles Dataset
. Contains details like:
      Title
      Type (Movie/TV Show)
      Genre
      Rating
      Release Year
      Country
      Duration

Dashboard Features

✔ Total Movies vs TV Shows
✔ Genre Distribution Analysis
✔ Content Rating Breakdown
✔ Country-wise Content Production
✔ Year-wise Content Growth Trend
✔ Interactive Filters for dynamic exploration

Key Insights
. Movies dominate Netflix’s catalog compared to TV Shows
. Significant growth in content production after 2015
. Drama & International Movies are among the most frequent genres
. USA leads in total content production
